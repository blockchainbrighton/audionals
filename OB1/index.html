About OB1

Ordinal Based 1's (OB1) is an innovative Bitcoin Ordinals project that uniquely combines 1/1 hand-drawn PFP (Profile Picture) art with an on-chain beat lab. Each distinct PFP within the collection features its own unique sound embedded directly within the inscription.

Launched as "the first Bitcoin beat lab and music studio," OB1 provides fundamental building blocks for artists and creators. It empowers users to generate music and audio content directly on the Bitcoin blockchain, leveraging the security and immutability of Ordinals while championing fair attribution and creator rights in the evolving digital landscape.

The Technology

Bitcoin Ordinals
Recursive Inscriptions
Audionals Protocol
On-Chain Metadata (JSON)

OB1 leverages cutting-edge Bitcoin blockchain technology, particularly Ordinals and Recursive Inscriptions, to create its revolutionary on-chain music platform:

  • Bitcoin Ordinals: Utilizes ordinal theory to inscribe data onto individual satoshis. Each satoshi gets a unique serial number, enabling tracking and unique asset creation directly on Bitcoin's mainnet.
  • Recursive Inscriptions: Overcomes Bitcoin's block size limitations (approx. 4MB via Taproot) by allowing inscriptions to request content from other inscriptions. This enables complex, large-scale projects like OB1's beat lab by referencing pre-inscribed code or sounds, reducing costs significantly.
  • Audionals Protocol: Developed by OB1 team member Jim, this protocol standardizes music inscription. It converts audio to Base64, embeds it in indexable JSON files, and includes rich metadata (key, tempo, creator).
  • On-Chain Storage & Metadata: Unlike many NFTs storing assets off-chain, OB1 aims for maximum on-chain permanence. Audio data and metadata are inscribed within the witness data of Bitcoin transactions, inheriting Bitcoin's security and immutability.

This technological stack allows OB1 to function as a decentralized, verifiable, and permanent music creation toolkit directly on the Bitcoin blockchain.

Music Lab

The OB1 Beat Lab is the core of the project – a functional, on-chain music creation environment built on Bitcoin. Each unique PFP NFT in the OB1 collection contains an embedded sound sample, forming a decentralized library of musical building blocks.

Leveraging the power of Recursive Inscriptions and the Audionals protocol, musicians and creators can access these sounds. The platform allows combining, layering, and sequencing these on-chain samples to produce new musical compositions. This process ensures that attribution is immutably recorded on the blockchain, paving the way for new models of collaborative music creation and fair royalty distribution.

Historical Significance

2014-2022

Early NFTs and Music

Music NFTs emerge, mainly on Ethereum, facing limitations like high fees, off-chain storage reliance, and file size constraints.

Jan 2023

Bitcoin Ordinals Protocol Launch

Casey Rodarmor launches Ordinals, enabling data inscription on individual satoshis, sparking NFT-like activity on Bitcoin.

Mid 2023

Recursive Inscriptions Breakthrough

Developers innovate recursive inscriptions, allowing inscriptions to fetch data from others, overcoming size limits and reducing costs.

Late 2023

OB1 Launches

OB1 establishes itself as "the first Bitcoin beat lab and music studio," leveraging Ordinals and recursive inscriptions for on-chain music creation.

Present

Pioneering On-Chain Music

OB1 continues to innovate, demonstrating a new model for music creation, ownership, attribution, and distribution directly on Bitcoin.

OB1 represents a significant milestone in the evolution of blockchain music. By successfully implementing the first on-chain beat lab on Bitcoin using Ordinals and recursive inscriptions, it demonstrated a practical solution to previous limitations. OB1 pioneers a new paradigm for musicians, offering tools for creation, immutable attribution, potential for new economic models, and direct artist-fan interaction, all secured by the Bitcoin network.
