What are Audionals?

Immutable Sound

Audionals are digital audio files (MP3, WAV, etc.) inscribed onto individual satoshis, the smallest unit of Bitcoin. This makes them part of Bitcoin's permanent, decentralized ledger.

True On-Chain Audio

Unlike many NFTs where metadata or the asset itself points to off-chain storage, Audionals store the actual audio data directly on the Bitcoin blockchain, ensuring unparalleled permanence.

Provable Ownership

Leveraging the Ordinals protocol, each Audional is uniquely identifiable and tied to a specific satoshi, allowing for verifiable ownership and transfer on the Bitcoin network.
