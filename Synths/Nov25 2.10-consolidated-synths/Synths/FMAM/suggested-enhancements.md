# Suggested Enhancements for FM KEYS

## Functionality
- **Algorithms:** Switch between different FM algorithms (Stack, Parallel) for different sound palettes.
- **LFO Matrix:** Independent LFOs for Pitch (Vibrato) and Timbre (Tremolo/Wah).
- **Arpeggiator:** Add a polyphonic arpeggiator.

## Creative Utility
- **Random Patch:** A "Randomize" button that intelligently generates usable FM sounds.
- **Macro Knobs:** XY Pad to control Harmonicity and Index simultaneously.

## User Experience
- **Envelope Graph:** Visual representation of the ADSR envelope.
- **Chord Memory:** One-finger chord triggering.
