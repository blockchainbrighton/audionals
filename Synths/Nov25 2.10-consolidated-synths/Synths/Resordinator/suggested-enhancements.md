# Suggested Enhancements for Resonator

## Functionality
- **Excitation Models:** Allow choosing between "Pluck", "Strike" (Mallet), or "Bow" (Sustained) excitation sources.
- **Body Impulse:** Use Convolution Reverb with Impulse Responses of real instrument bodies (Guitar, Violin) for realistic resonance.

## Creative Utility
- **Strum Mode:** A "Strum" button that plays the held chord with a slight delay between notes, mimicking a guitar strum.
- **Microtuning:** Support for non-western tunings to explore sitar/oud styles.

## User Experience
- **String Tension:** A visual control to adjust the "tightness" or "looseness" of the strings (Pitch decay).
