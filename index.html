On-Chain Composition

Our fully on-chain Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) lets you compose directly into Bitcoin itself. Every synth, sample and pattern is stored immutably, making your music part of the blockchain‚Äôs permanent history.

Our flagship piece TRUTH shows what this unlocks: a full-length, studio-quality track composed, rendered and replayable entirely from on-chain instructions.

The Merkle Tree of Music

Each time you save your project, you update its Merkle tree‚Äîa cryptographic family tree of the song. This ledger, embedded within the file, proves exactly when your creation was born and who holds the keys to its origin.

Recursive Creation & Fair Attribution

The future of music is collaborative. When another artist remixes your work, the Audionals protocol ensures your contribution is never lost. The new song‚Äôs ledger automatically references the original, preserving your legacy and making attribution a mathematical certainty.

Created with the Audional Sequencer, TRUTH is an 8-minute, uncompressed, studio-quality composition generated entirely from a 3kb JSON sequencing file. Rather than storing bulk audio on-chain, TRUTH demonstrates a path that treats block space as sacred: using minimal, reusable, deterministic instructions to regenerate a full WAV-resolution track from just 3kb of Bitcoin block space.
