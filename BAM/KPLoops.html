About Kieron Pepper

Kieron Pepper is a multi-instrumentalist and former live drummer for electronic music pioneers The Prodigy (1997–2007), renowned for his powerful, high-energy performances. A current member of the legendary BBC Radiophonic Workshop—most recently collaborating with Spitfire Audio—Kieron is also the bass player for Britpop icons Sleeper, co-writer and co-producer with Wargasm, and Director of Artist Development at WaterBear College of Music.

Now an accomplished producer and dedicated educator, Kieron brings decades of rhythmic innovation to the Bitcoin Audional Matrix (BAM) with this exclusive set of 25 brand-new, electrifying drum loops designed for the future of on-chain music creation.

Inscribed via Audionals Protocol

Immutable & On-Chain

Each Kieron Pepper drum loop is meticulously packaged as a JSON-based Audionals asset. The high-quality audio is encoded using Opus or Base64 and inscribed directly onto individual satoshis using the Ordinals protocol.

This ensures verifiable, immutable ownership and enables seamless playback directly from the Bitcoin blockchain, pioneering a new standard for digital audio assets.

Audional Standard

Built upon the Audional protocol, these loops contain rich metadata within their inscriptions, describing tempo, key (if applicable), genre tags, and creator information, all stored immutably on-chain.

This structured data paves the way for future integration into on-chain DAWs and decentralized music applications.
