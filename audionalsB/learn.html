Your guide to navigating the world of Audionals and on-chain music.

How to Get Involved

1

Get a Compatible Wallet

You need a Bitcoin wallet that supports Ordinals to hold Audionals. See the Ecosystem section below for options.

Find a Wallet ↓
2

Acquire Bitcoin (BTC)

You'll need BTC in your wallet to potentially purchase Audional components/tracks and pay for Bitcoin network transaction fees (for inscribing or transferring).

3

Explore Audionals

Discover existing Audional components and compositions on marketplaces or via Audional explorers (once available).

Explore Marketplaces ↓
4

Start Creating

Use the Audional Sequencer (when available) and other tools to create your own music on-chain.
