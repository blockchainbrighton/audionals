Audional Sequencer - B64X Details

The Audional Sequencer (B64X) represents a paradigm shift in music creation. Instead of saving project files to a local disk, your entire composition structure—which samples play when, their arrangement, effect parameters—is inscribed directly onto the Bitcoin blockchain as an Ordinal.

Key features include:

  • Loading and sequencing inscribed Audional Components (samples/loops).
  • Grid-based pattern editing familiar to DAW users.
  • Basic on-chain effect parameter storage (e.g., volume, pan, simple filter settings).
  • Immutable, verifiable proof of your creative process stored on Bitcoin.
  • Foundation for transparent collaboration and rights management.

OrdSPD Sample Pad Details

OrdSPD transforms the concept of a sample pad into an interactive, on-chain experience. Load Ordinal-inscribed audio samples onto pads and trigger them to create beats and textures. Its regenerative capabilities allow for evolving soundscapes based on blockchain data or user interaction.

Highlights:

  • Load Audional Components (samples) onto interactive pads.
  • Real-time triggering and performance.
  • Potential for generative/algorithmic behavior linked to on-chain events.
  • Ideal for live performance or dynamic beat creation using verified on-chain sounds.
  • Explore remixes and mashups of existing on-chain audio elements.
