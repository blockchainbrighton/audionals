A Living History in Every File

In the old world, a song is a static file. Tracking who played the bassline or who owns the sample is a mess of paperwork. Audionals changes the paradigm.

  • Blockchain Secured Merkle Trees Every time you save your work, it becomes part of the song's cryptographic DNA. The file itself contains its entire history.
  • Single Source of Truth The JSON file created by our sequencers is an on-chain artifact. It remembers who did what, and which plugins were used.
  • Forkable & Mutable When someone remixes your track, the history forks. The new song remembers the original contributors in the ledger, automatically protecting attribution.

Royalty Without The Rulers

By chaining save file changes to the Bitcoin ledger, we create a transparent ecosystem where the song is the contract.

1

Compose On-Chain

Use the Audional Sequencer to write to the chain. Your samples, synths, and patterns are hashed and stored forever.

2

Save the DNA

Every save updates the Merkle tree. The ledger proves exactly when the file was made and who owns the wallet that made it.

3

Fork & Earn

Future artists can remix your JSON files. Because the history is in the file, your contribution is preserved in the new song's metadata.
