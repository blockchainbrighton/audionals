How to Play

  • Place mirrors and splitters to guide the laser beam from the top-left corner to all green targets
  • Select a component from the palette, then click on the grid to place it
  • Right-click to remove components or use the Clear tool
  • Mirror / reflects the laser clockwise, Mirror \ reflects counter-clockwise
  • Splitters create a new beam at 90° while letting the original beam pass through
  • Win by illuminating all targets simultaneously
