    Welcome to the Museum of Ordinals

    This museum houses 68 significant Bitcoin Ordinals, spanning from the very first inscription to revolutionary collections that defined digital artifact culture on Bitcoin. Each piece is preserved immutably on the Bitcoin blockchain.

    Use the guided tour to explore chronologically, browse by collection, or jump to random artifacts. Every item includes historical context, creator information, and technical details.
