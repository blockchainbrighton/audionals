Project Synopsis

AudioGNARLS is a collection of 256 seed-driven synth entities and the world's first fully on-chain, programmable VST audio synthesizer inscribed on Bitcoin. Each of the 256 Gnarls produces unique oscilloscope art, a bank of 18 deterministic presets, and a reproducible audio call sign, bridging the legacy of classic hardware with the future of on-chain music creation.

Quick Facts

  • What: 256 ownable on-chain synth presets (VST instrument).
  • Network: Bitcoin (via Ordinals).
  • Tribute: Numbered 0-255 to honor MIDI's 8-bit universe.
  • Price: 0.0000255 BTC.
  • Vision: The foundation of the Audionals protocol, a future blockchain-native Digital Audio Workstation (DAW).
