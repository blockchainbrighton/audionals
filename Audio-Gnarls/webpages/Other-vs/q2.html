AUDIOGNARLS

The first on-chain synthesizer collection, inscribed forever on Bitcoin.

What Just Happened

As announced by Ord.io, the world’s first interactive audio synthesizer has been fully inscribed on-chain. This milestone unlocks a new frontier: electronic music creation, natively on Bitcoin.

About the Collection

AudioGNARLS is a series of 256 seed-driven synth entities. Each Gnarl generates:

Unique Oscilloscope Artwork

Visual representations of each Gnarl's sonic signature, permanently etched on-chain.

18 Deterministic Presets

A complete bank of reproducible sounds that define each Gnarl's character.

Reproducible Audio Signature

Each Gnarl's unique "call sign" — a sonic fingerprint that can be verified and reproduced.

These are not 8-bit throwbacks. They are modern, procedural synth presets, designed with a custom Tone.js engine featuring oscillators, filters, envelopes, and modulation. Each Gnarl's sound and art are permanently etched on-chain, inseparable and eternal.

From Collectible to Instrument

AudioGNARLS is both art and instrument:

  • Import any Gnarl's sound bank into the upcoming AudioGNARLS Sequencer (BVST synth).
  • Use the Ordinal ID of your Gnarl to load its unique presets.
  • Tracks created with those sounds will credit the Gnarl's owner — linking provenance and authorship at the protocol level.

Why It Matters

This is more than a drop. It's the foundation of the AudioGNARLS protocol — a blockchain-native digital audio workstation (DAW) where:

  • Presets are provably unique and ownable.
  • Rights and recognition are built directly into sound design.
  • Art, code, and music converge into a living ecosystem.
