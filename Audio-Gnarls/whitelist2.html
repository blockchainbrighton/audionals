Key Talking Points

Want to write your own post? Here are the essential details.

  • What It Is: The first-ever ownable, programmable BVST on-chain instrument.
  • The Collection: 256 unique synth presets, numbered 0-255.
  • The Homage: A tribute to MIDI's 8-bit universe and the iconic SH-101 synthesizer.
  • The Price: 0.0000255 BTC per synth.
  • The Tech: Each synth generates unique oscilloscope art, 18 presets, and a reproducible audio signature, all inscribed on Bitcoin.
  • The Vision: The foundation for the Audionals protocol, a future blockchain-native digital audio workstation (DAW).
