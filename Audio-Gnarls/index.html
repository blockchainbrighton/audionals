About the Collection

AudioGNARLS is a collection of 256 seed-driven BVST synth entities. Each is uniquely identified by a number from 0 to 255, a direct homage to the 8-bit universe that MIDI inhabits.

The collection is also a nod to the iconic SH-101 synthesizer, a classic instrument whose sonic character influenced the AudioGNARLS sound engine. This project bridges the legacy of beloved hardware with the future of on-chain music.

Together, these influences are encoded into the identity of each piece. The full inscription name for every Gnarl ends with b101i# followed by its unique number. The 'b' stands for blockchain, marking this as the first Blockchain hosted VST (Virtual Studio Tool) instrument designed for the new on-chain music environment of the future. The '101' is a tribute to the SH-101, culminating in a series that spans the complete MIDI range from b101i#0 to b101i#255.

Each of the 256 Gnarls generates:

Unique oscilloscope artwork

Procedural visuals paired 1:1 with sound.

Bank of 18 deterministic presets

Modern patches — oscillators, filters, envelopes, modulation.

Reproducible audio signature

A call sign that’s always the same from the same seed.
