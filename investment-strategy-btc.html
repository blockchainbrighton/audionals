Executive Summary

Moving  BTC into sBTC (a 1:1 Bitcoin-backed asset on the Stacks layer) lets you earn ~5 % APY in BTC while keeping full self-custody with your Ledger. The yield is paid natively by the Stacks protocol (no lending risk) and the peg is secured by a decentralized threshold-signer network (70 % consensus). An extra 5-7 % can be harvested by lending a portion through Zest Protocol (vetted borrowers, KYC’d counterparties).

Key facts. sBTC launched Dec 2024. First 1 000 BTC cap filled in two days; cap raised to 3 000 BTC Feb 2025. Withdrawals enabled from Mar 2025 onward.

Strategy Deep-Dive

1. Direct sBTC Peg (Base Yield)

Convert BTC → sBTC via the trust-minimized bridge, then simply hold in a Ledger-controlled wallet. Rewards (~5 % APY) arrive automatically every PoX cycle (~2 weeks). Zero lending risk, zero impermanent loss.

2. Zest Lending Pool (~12.5 % APY)

Deposit sBTC into Zest’s Earn Pool. Interest is paid by institutional borrowers (market-makers, trading desks) that undergo KYC and post collateral. Expect 10–12.5 % APY, lock-up ~6–12 months, moderate counter-party risk.

3. sBTC–STX DEX Liquidity (30-60 % APY)

Provide equal USD value of sBTC and STX to Bitflow/Velar pools. Earn trading fees + liquidity-mining rewards. High headline APY but impermanent loss can erode BTC-denominated principal.

4. Borrow & Farm (Advanced, 25 % APY)

Use sBTC as collateral to mint USDh, then stake USDh in Hermetica for ~25 % APY. Requires active management, liquidation risk if BTC drops, and synthetic-stablecoin peg risk. Not recommended under the “no principal loss” mandate.

Recommended Plan & Rationale

Anchor (80–100 %): Hold the bulk of the BTC in pure sBTC to lock in the ~5 % BTC-native yield with minimal risk. Optional Booster (0–20 %): If comfortable with vetted counter-party exposure, allocate up to 20 % to Zest’s sBTC lending pool to target a blended ~7–8 % APY.

This allocation keeps principal protection front-of-mind while still materially exceeding the ≥5 % target.

Step-by-Step Implementation Checklist

  • Wallet Setup. Install Xverse or Leather, connect Ledger, and verify the Stacks address is hardware-derived.
  • Test Run. Send 0.01 BTC via the app.stacks.co bridge to confirm flow and timing (≈ 10–30 min).
  • Full Deposit. Bridge the remaining BTC → receive sBTC (after 80 k sats consolidation fee).
  • Secure Storage. Leave sBTC in Ledger-controlled address. Rewards arrive automatically every PoX cycle (~2 weeks).
  • (Optional) Zest Allocation. Supply sBTC (~20 %) to Zest Earn Pool; note loan-cycle end-date on calendar.
  • Monitoring. Mid-year check: verify reward accrual (wallet / explorer) and review Zest dashboard for any borrower alerts.
  • Exit (after ~12 mo). Withdraw from Zest → consolidate sBTC → peg-out via bridge → native BTC + yield lands back in your L1 Ledger.
