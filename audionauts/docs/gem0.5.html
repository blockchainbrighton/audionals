Technical Manifest

  • 100% On-Chain Assets — Animations, vector images, audio, and all metadata are permanently inscribed as Bitcoin Ordinals. No off-chain dependencies.
  • Gasless Playback — All assets stream directly from the decentralized Ordinals index, requiring no IPFS gateways or centralized servers.
  • Open Source Tooling — Our free Opus File Generator app empowers any musician to compress and inscribe their own on-chain audio.
  • Future-Proof Licensing — All art and audio utilize a CC-BY-SA-Web3 license, built to encourage sampling, remixing, and commercial releases within the BAM ecosystem.
