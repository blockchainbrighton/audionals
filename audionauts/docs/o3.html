8. FAQ

Do I need a Bitcoin wallet to mint?

Yes — any Ordinals-compatible wallet (e.g., Xverse or Unisat) will work. Minting happens directly on-chain.

Will the audio stream in all browsers?

Opus is widely supported. For older Safari versions we provide an automatic fallback to AAC.

Is commercial use really allowed?

Absolutely. The CC-BY-SA-Web3 licence lets you sample, remix, and monetise as long as you credit and share alike.
