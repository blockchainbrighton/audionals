The Collection: 888 Keys to the Matrix

Audionauts is your definitive entry point into the Bitcoin Audional Matrix (BAM). The collection’s 888 inscriptions are a direct nod to the 88-sample format of the first sample library, KP Loops, and all future BAM drops. This number connects the 88 keys of a piano to the control panels of a spacecraft. Holding an Audionaut reserves your priority access for every subsequent 88-piece mission.

Rarity Snapshot

  • Legendary (≈1%): Top-tier animation × badge combinations that appear only once across the entire collection.
  • Epic (≈10%): Distinctive combinations limited to a maximum of 10 mints each.
  • Rare/Common (≈89%): A balanced distribution designed for accessibility while ensuring every Audionaut remains unique.

All rarity is deterministic, generated from on-chain data, and fully verifiable by anyone.

Technical Specifications

  • 100% On-Chain & Immutable: All assets—animations, vector art, audio, and metadata—are inscribed directly on Bitcoin. No external servers, no IPFS, no broken links.
  • Zero-Gas Playback: Once inscribed, assets stream directly from the chain to your browser at no cost to the user.
  • Opus Toolkit Ready: Empowering the next wave of artists with our free Opus File Generator to compress audio for efficient on-chain storage.
  • Web3-Native Licensing: A CC-BY-SA-Web3 license encourages sampling, remixing, and commercial use within the BAM ecosystem.
